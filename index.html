Tremco Construction

Client: Tremco Construction | Role: Webflow Developer | Year: 2024

Overview

Tremco Construction needed a high‑performance website to showcase their industrial projects. I built the entire site in Webflow with custom Lenis smooth scroll integration, ensuring a premium feel and fast load times.

Challenge

The site had heavy imagery and complex layouts. The challenge was to maintain smooth scrolling and animations without compromising performance.

Solution

Used Webflow's native CMS for scalability, implemented Lenis for buttery scroll, and optimized all assets. Added subtle GSAP animations on key sections.

Technologies used

WebflowLenisGSAPFigma

Results

Client reported increased engagement and a 35% drop in bounce rate. The smooth scroll became a key selling point.
